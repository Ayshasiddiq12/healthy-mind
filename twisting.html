Finding Stillness in Motion


Introduction

Twisting poses are a vital part of yoga and fitness routines that involve gently rotating the spine and torso. These movements aren’t just about flexibility — they activate the core, stimulate internal organs, and leave your entire body feeling refreshed and renewed.Twists help "wring out" physical tension and emotional stress, much like wringing water from a cloth. Whether seated, standing, or lying down, each twist helps restore spinal alignment, improve circulation, and bring balance to the body and mind.Perfect for people with sedentary lifestyles or those seeking detox and mental clarity, twisting poses combine breath and movement to awaken inner strength and calm.

  • Stimulates digestion by massaging internal organs
  • Detoxifies the body by enhancing circulation
  • Reduces back and neck pain
  • Improves posture and body alignment
  • Calms the nervous system, helping reduce stress
  • Improves Digestive Health

How to Practice


  • Start with a neutral spine either seated, standing, or lying down.
  • Inhale to lengthen your spine, creating space between vertebrae.
  • Exhale and gently twist, starting from your lower spine up through your shoulders and neck..
  • Keep your hips stable and twist with awareness — never force it..
  • Hold for 3–5 breaths, then slowly return to center and switch sides..

When and Where

Ideal during mid-morning or evening sessions, or after sitting for long hours Practice in a calm, quiet space with a yoga mat. Can be done indoors or outdoors.
